A Blog About Me

By Cristian Bahena



About Myself:

My name is Cristian Bahena and I am 22 years old. I have an Associate of Arts Degree from the College of Lake County.
Before I began programming I was interested in pursing a degree in Marketing since I had always done sales in the past.
After I received my Associates Degree I realized I wanted to become a software engineer. I began my journey with Codecademy.

Why Actualize And Not A University?

I decided not to pursue my Bachelors Degree in Marketing after I saw my brother become a software engineer.
He graduated from Actualize about a year or two ago and realized he got the same outcome as those that got a 4 year degree.
He was the one that got me into programming and made me realized it was something I enjoyed. Shortly after I decided to pursue a similar path!

My Journey So Far:

After my degree I tried school for a little while longer and realized they made it so hard to learn coding in school.
I decided to teach myself by using Codecademy. Through the resources they provided I found it easy to learn code.
I once felt that I would never understand it but a few months later I now have a certification on the basics of HTML, CSS, Python, and JavaScript.
