Ingredients:

  • 100 Chicken Breast
  • 100 Cup of Cheese
  • 300 Can of Tomato Sauce
  • 10 Can of Bread Crumbs
  • 30 Eggs

Directions:

  1. Pre-heat oven to 1000 degrees fahrenheit.
  2. Beat your 30 eggs and pour into a bowl.
  3. Dip your 100 chicken breasts into the eggs.
  4. Cover the 100 chicken breasts in bread crumbs.
  5. Now put in oven.
  6. Once baked put tomato sauce on top of each chicken breast.
  7. Lastly put cheese over the sauce and put back in oven for 10 minutes.
